About Me

Hello! My name is Taylor Sanders. I am 27 years old and currently live in Anchorage, Alaska. I am originally from a very small town South Carolina, called York. I enjoy hiking & fishing here in beautiful Alaska! Baking is my favorite hobby. I have been a pastry chef for the past 7 years - and baking long before that. It is my passion. I also studied pastry in Paris, France for a year which was an amazing experience. When the pandemic hit I came to the realization that I needed a more stable career. Web Development sparked my interest in creativity. I am really enjoying learning how computers work and using my skills to create beautiful things. I am excited to see what the future holds and look forward to becoming a Full Stack Web Developer!
